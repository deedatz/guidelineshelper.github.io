Common Requests

Physical Therapy

Find all things related to physical therapy. Whether it's postoperative, regular, or "chronic" PT this is the table to look up. Also includes Work Hardening, Work conditioning, and FCE.

Medications

Zanaflex request giving you spasms? Getting overdosed with Norco reviews? Topical analgesics getting under your skin? Find your guidelines here.

Diagnostics

MRI, EMG/NCVs, CTScans, Genetic Testing, Laboratory Testing that's not for surgery.

Injections

Facet Injections, MBB, ESIs, Rhizotomy/Neurotomy/RFA, Alcohol Injections, Trigger Point Injections, Botox Injections, Injections! Injections! Injections!

Surgeries

Surgeries and related services. Yes, this includes Preop clearance and Labs, Postop DMEs and even a repeat appearance of Post Op PT.

Equipment

Medical equipment that is not necessarily postoperative. Includes your cold/hot packs, splints, wheelchair, mattress/bed, TENS, IF, HWave, ironman suit... alright, maybe not that last one.

Other Requests

Requests that do not fall into the above categories. Generic requests for consults, Home Health Services, Transportations, House and lot.
